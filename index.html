Our Story

We first met as classmates in Tacurong National High School, but it was during our 4th year that something truly special began to bloom between us. From classmates to friends, and then to a couple, we started a journey together that would last 13 amazing years.

Through all the laughter, challenges, and countless adventures, our love only grew stronger. We cheered each other on, supported each other’s dreams, and found comfort knowing we could always count on one another.

Today, we are overjoyed to start a new chapter — one filled with love, commitment, and the promise of a lifetime together. What began as friendship in high school has blossomed into a love story that we are proud to share with all of you.
